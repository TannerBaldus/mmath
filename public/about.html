About

For those unfamiliar MMA Math is the term for trying to apply the transative property to MMA. For example: Fighter A beat Fighter B and Fighter B beat Fighter C; therefore Fighter A can beat fighter C. This almost never works out as fighting is a series stylistic match ups rather than a neatly orderd ranking. This site should show how silly MMA MATH is and hopefully provide some laughs.
